Academic Background

I am Sandra Vanessa M. Villanueva, a 3rd year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at STI College Fairview. My academic journey in IT has given me a strong foundation in web development, database management, programming logic, and system design.


Throughout my studies, I have consistently applied both theoretical knowledge and hands-on practice to develop real-world solutions. My coursework has covered a wide range of technologies, from front-end web design to back-end development and database integration.

Beyond academics, I am someone who values creativity, precision, and continuous learning. I enjoy exploring new technologies and finding elegant solutions to complex problems. I believe that great software is not just functional — it's also intuitive and visually compelling.


I am driven by the idea that technology can bridge gaps and create opportunities for people, and I aspire to be part of that positive change through the work I do as a future IT professional.
